Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Pro before buying?

Yes! Pro comes with a 7-day free trial. No charge until the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime during the trial.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Your data is always preserved. If you downgrade from Pro, you'll keep all existing data but won't be able to add more than 4 miners or access Pro-only features.

Is HashWatch open source?

Yes! HashWatch is MIT licensed and fully open source. You can self-host the backend or use our cloud service.

Which miners are supported?

HashWatch supports all BitAxe models (Ultra, Max, Gamma) and any miner with a local API endpoint.

How do I pay?

On mobile, pay via App Store / Google Play (in-app purchase). On web, pay via Stripe. Same subscription, synced across platforms.

Can I switch between plans?

Absolutely. Upgrade from Free to Pro anytime. Downgrade from Pro to Free and keep your data — just with the 4-miner limit.
