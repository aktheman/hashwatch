Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Email address and password (stored as bcrypt hash).

Miner Data: IP addresses, names, and tags you assign to your miners. Hashtate, temperature, power, and performance metrics collected from your miners.

Usage Data: Screen views, feature usage, and error events (stored locally and optionally sent to our servers).

Device Information: Platform (iOS/Android/Web), OS version, and app version.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: (a) provide and improve the Service; (b) send alerts and notifications you've configured; (c) process subscriptions; (d) detect and prevent abuse.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored in PostgreSQL databases hosted on Railway (US). Miner data is also stored locally on your device via SQLite. We do not sell your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except: (a) payment processors (Apple, Google, Stripe) for subscription management; (b) when required by law.

5. Dark Pool

If you opt into the Dark Pool, aggregated and anonymized hashrate data is contributed to a network-wide statistics pool. Individual miner identities are never exposed.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including JWT authentication, encrypted connections (HTTPS), input validation, and rate limiting.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

Privacy questions? Email privacy@hashwatch.app.
