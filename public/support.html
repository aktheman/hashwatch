Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add my first miner?

Open the app, tap the + button, and enter your miner's IP address. HashWatch will auto-discover it on your local network.

My miner shows as offline

Check that your miner is powered on and connected to the same network. Try pinging the IP address. If it's a new miner, it may take a few minutes to appear.

How do push notifications work?

On mobile, HashWatch uses Expo push notifications. On web, it uses VAPID push. Enable notifications in Settings → Notifications.

Can I self-host the backend?

Yes! See the README for Docker setup instructions. You'll need PostgreSQL and Node.js 22+.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

On iOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. On Android: Play Store → Subscriptions. On web: Contact us.
